N-Grams in NLP

An N-gram means a sequence of N words; a continuous sequences of words or symbols or tokens in a document. They may be used for sentence auto completion (such as in Gmail /Outlook), auto spell check, and to a certain extent, for grammar in a given sentence. 

sentence = 'I came by car'

Tokens:['I', 'came', 'by', 'car'] # 4 tokens in sentence
unigrams = 'I', 'came', 'by', 'car'
bigrams = 'I came', 'came by', 'by car'
trigrams = 'I came by', 'came by car'
